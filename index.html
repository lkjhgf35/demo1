Who We Are

Camden's oldest masjid — serving the community since 1985.

The Quba School & Islamic Center holds the distinct honor of being the oldest masjid in the city of Camden, NJ. For over four decades, we have been a beacon of faith, knowledge, and community for Muslims and neighbors throughout the region.

We are proud promoters of the Qur'aan and the Sunnah, upholding the tradition of our Righteous Ancestors. Whether you are new to Islam or a lifelong Muslim, there is a place for you here.
