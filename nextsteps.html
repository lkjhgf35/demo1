New to Quba

Plan Your Visit

All are welcome at Quba. Here is everything you need to know before your first visit.

Step 01

Find Us

We are located at 1311 Haddon Ave, Camden, NJ. Street parking is available nearby. We are accessible by public transit. Call us at +1 (856) 870-8170 if you need directions.

Step 02

Attend Jumu'ah

Jumu'ah (Friday Prayer) is at 1:00 PM. Arrive by 12:45 PM for the khutbah. Men and women are both welcome. Dress modestly and perform wudu before arriving if possible.

Step 03

Meet the Community

After prayer, stay and introduce yourself. Our brothers and sisters are warm and welcoming. Whether you are a lifelong Muslim or exploring Islam for the first time, you will be received with openness.

Step 04

Get Involved

Once you feel at home, explore our programs — Quranic studies, the Islamic school, weekend classes, adult halaqas, and more. There is a place for everyone at Quba regardless of your level of knowledge.

What to Expect at Jumu'ah

The Friday prayer begins with the imam delivering the khutbah (sermon) in two parts, followed by the congregational salah. The entire service typically lasts 30–45 minutes. Wudu facilities are available on-site. Women are welcome and have a designated prayer area. There is no dress code enforced, but modest dress is appreciated — for men, covered shoulders and long pants; for women, modest clothing and a headscarf if Muslim.

Interested in Taking Your Shahada?

If you are considering becoming Muslim or have recently taken your shahada, we would love to welcome you into the community and support your journey. Reach out to us — we are here for you.
