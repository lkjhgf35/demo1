Quranic Studies

Memorization (hifz), recitation (tajweed), and understanding of the Holy Qur'aan for students of all ages. Whether you are just beginning or seeking to complete your memorization, our qualified teachers guide you every step of the way.

Weekend School

Adult Education

Regular halaqas, Islamic lectures, and study circles for adult community members. Topics include aqeedah, fiqh, tazkiyah, and the sciences of the Qur'aan. Knowledge is an obligation upon every Muslim.
