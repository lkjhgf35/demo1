Our Story

About Quba

Established 1985 — the oldest masjid in Camden, NJ, rooted in the Qur'aan and Sunnah.

Our Mission

Promoters of the Qur'aan, the Sunnah, and the tradition of our Righteous Ancestors.

The Quba School & Islamic Center was founded in 1985 with a clear purpose: to establish a place of worship, learning, and community grounded firmly in the Qur'aan and the authentic Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, upon the understanding of the Salaf as-Salih — our Righteous Predecessors.

We hold the distinct honor of being the oldest masjid in the city of Camden, New Jersey. For over four decades, we have served as a spiritual home and educational institution for Muslims throughout Camden and the surrounding region.

Our History

Four decades of faith in Camden

The Quba School & Islamic Center was established in 1985 by a dedicated group of Muslims who recognized the need for a masjid rooted in authentic Islamic knowledge in the heart of Camden. From humble beginnings, Quba has grown into a full-service Islamic center offering worship, education, and community programs for all ages.

Today, as the oldest masjid in Camden, we carry that original vision forward — providing a place where Muslims can deepen their faith, children can receive a sound Islamic education, and all people can find a welcoming community grounded in the truth of Islam.
